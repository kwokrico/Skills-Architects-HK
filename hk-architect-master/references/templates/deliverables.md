## 13. Presentation rules (HK practice)

- **One pack = one purpose** — do not mix For Approval and For Construction in one transmittal.
- **Declare exclusions** — prevents more disputes than extra drawing content.
- **Stable sheet numbering** — renumber only with transmittal explanation.
- **Scale bar on every drawing** — prints get resized; stated scale alone is unreliable.
- **Interface notes at trade boundaries** — state what is by whom and where the line sits.
- **Audience-first packaging** — contractor = buildable; client = decision-led; authority = compliance-indexed.
- **Bilingual labels** — use English for contractual/issue documents; Chinese summaries optional for client boards where helpful.
