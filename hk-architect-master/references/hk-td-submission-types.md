# TD / HyD submission types — traffic coordination

High-level map for **planning TIA** vs **construction TMP**. Verify current TD/HyD forms and processing times on live projects.

| Submission type | Stage | Typical authority | Purpose | Typical lead time |
|---------------|-------|-------------------|---------|-------------------|
| Traffic Impact Assessment (TIA) | Planning / s.16 | PlanD condition → TD review | Assess development traffic generation, parking, access | 2–4 months (with consultant study) |
| Temporary Traffic Management Plan (TMP) | Construction lane 1+ | TD | Hoarding, haul routes, pedestrian diversion | 4–8 weeks |
| Lane occupation / road closure | Construction | TD | Crane lifts, concrete pours, oversize delivery | 2–6 weeks per event |
| Bus stop relocation / suspension | Construction / planning | TD + operator | Maintain public transport during works | 8–12 weeks |
| Loading / unloading bay impact study | Planning or construction | TD | Servicing strategy for tower/podium | With TIA or TMP |
| Highway works / connection (public road) | External works → CC | HyD | New public road, footpath, street furniture | Months — align `hk-certificate-of-compliance` |
| Green Area road handover | Completion | HyD | As-built roads within grant | CC package |

**TD vs HyD rule of thumb:**

- **TD** — traffic operations on existing public roads during construction; TIA/TMP; bus stops; temporary arrangements.
- **HyD** — design and handover of **public highway assets** (roads, footpaths, slopes at highway interface) under grant/CC.

**Architect coordination:** Appoint traffic consultant early if s.16 condition or site frontage on busy carriageway. Link hoarding design (`hk-site-establishment`) to TMP before hoarding erection.
