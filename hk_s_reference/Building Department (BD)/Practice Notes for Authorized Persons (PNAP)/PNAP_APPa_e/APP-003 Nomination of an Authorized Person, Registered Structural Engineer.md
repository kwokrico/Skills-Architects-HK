APP-3

Nomination of an Authorized Person,
Registered Structural Engineer or Registered Geotechnical Engineer
to act in stead

When an authorized person (AP), registered structural engineer (RSE) or
registered  geotechnical  engineer  (RGE),  who  has  been  appointed  in  respect  of  building
works,  street  works  or  minor  works,  is  incapacitated  by  illness  or  proposes  to  be
temporarily absent from Hong Kong, a temporary replacement should be nominated by
submission  of  the  specified  form,  i.e.  Form  BA  21  or  Form  MW09,  within  7  days.
Sections  4(2)  and  4A(5)  of  the  Buildings  Ordinance,  Regulation  23(2)  of  the  Building
(Administration)  Regulations  and  section  49  of  the  Building  (Minor  Works)  Regulation
refer.

To avoid ambiguity and abuse of the Buildings Ordinance, the Building
Authority  (BA)  considers  that  an  acting  period  exceeding  3  months  or  at  frequent
