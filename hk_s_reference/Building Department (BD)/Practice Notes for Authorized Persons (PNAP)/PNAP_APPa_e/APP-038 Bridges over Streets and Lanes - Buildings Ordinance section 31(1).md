Bridges over Streets and Lanes
Buildings Ordinance section 31(1)

There  are  occasions  when  bridges  may  have  to  be  constructed  over
streets/lanes.    A  bridge  may  link  buildings  which  have  integrated  functions  and/or
associated  interests,  or  may  be  required  by  the  Director  of  Lands  under  the  terms  of  the
lease.
