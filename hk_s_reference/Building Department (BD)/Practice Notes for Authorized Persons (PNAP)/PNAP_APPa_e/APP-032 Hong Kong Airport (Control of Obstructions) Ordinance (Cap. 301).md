  Hong Kong Airport (Control of Obstructions) Ordinance (Cap. 301)

Airport Height Restrictions

The Hong Kong Airport (Control of Obstructions) Ordinance (Cap. 301)
provides  for,  among  others,  the  restriction  of  heights  of  buildings  in  the  interest  of
ensuring  aviation  safety.    The  airport  height  restrictions  (AHRs)  vary  at  different
locations of Hong Kong and these height limits are set out in a series of Airport Height
Restriction Plans which are deposited in the Land Registry and can be purchased from
the Map Publications Centres of the Lands Department.  For details, please refer to the
website of Civil Aviation Department at www.cad.gov.hk.
