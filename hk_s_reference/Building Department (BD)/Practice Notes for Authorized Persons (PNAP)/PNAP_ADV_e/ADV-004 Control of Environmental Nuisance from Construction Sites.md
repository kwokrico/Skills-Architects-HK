Practice Note for Authorized Persons,
Buildings Department Registered Structural Engineers and ADV-4
Registered Geotechnical Engineers
Control of Environmental Nuisance from Construction Sites
Construction sites if not properly managed may become a source of
environmental nuisance and pollution affecting not only the workers on site but also
adjoining occupants and the general public. Authorized persons, registered structural
engineers and registered geotechnical engineers are therefore strongly urged to take
appropriate steps to prevent or minimise the impact of construction activities on the
surrounding environment.
2. A checklist with practical advice for the control of environmental nuisance
from construction sites is provided in Appendix A. Further guidance, if required, may
be sought from the relevant Government departments as listed.
3. A similar practice note has been issued to registered contractors.
