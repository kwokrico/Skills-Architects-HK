191

Display of Site Information

To enable members of the public to bring to the attention of Authorised
Persons/Registered Structural Engineers (AP/RSE) any undesirable or unsafe conditions
on building sites, you are requested to provide relevant information on your building site
to  facilitate  contact.    It  is  desirable  that  the  following  information  be  displayed  in  a
prominent position on site for public information :

a)  Address and/or lot number of the site;
b)  Names of AP, RSE and registered contractor;
c)  Contact telephone number(s).

While display of advertisement is not permitted on any hoarding or covered
2.
walkway,  there  is  no  objection  under  Building  (Planning)  Regulation  66(4)  for  the
display  of  the  basic  development  information  such  as  proposed  works,  use,  height,
number of blocks and the anticipated project completion date.

3.
For information displayed in locations other than the hoarding and covered
walkway, you should ensure that such information is displayed within lot boundaries and
does not interfere with the site activities.

4.

A similar practice note has been issued to registered contractors.
