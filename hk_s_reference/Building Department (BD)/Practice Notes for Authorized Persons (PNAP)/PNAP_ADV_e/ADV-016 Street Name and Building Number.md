Street Name and Building Number

Proper  display  of  building  numbers  can  facilitate  identification  of
buildings for the provision of emergency, postal delivery and other government services
as well as for the convenience of visiting public and tourists.

2.
Under  section 32(2)  of  the  Buildings  Ordinance,  the  Commissioner  of
Rating and Valuation (CRV) is empowered to allocate building numbers and prescribe
the manner in which such numbers should be marked for the purpose of distinguishing
such  building.    The  CRV  or  the  Building  Authority  (BA)  may  order  the  owner  of  a
building  to  mark  the  building  with  the  allocated  building  numbers  in  the  prescribed
manner.

Allocation of Building Number and Guidelines for Displaying Building Number

3.
The CRV has issued a practice note setting out the principles on allocation
of building numbers and guidelines for displaying building numbers.  You are advised to
make  reference  to  the  practice  note  which  can  be  viewed  and  downloaded  from  the
Rating and Valuation Department (RVD) webpage :-

English version : http://www.rvd.gov.hk/en/issues/index.htm
Chinese version : http://www.rvd.gov.hk/tc/issues/index.htm

4.
In respect of the manner of displaying building number, it is stated in
RVD’s Practice Note 1/2007 that the building number should be clearly displayed above
or at the side of the entrance to the premises.  The colour of the number or number plate
should contrast clearly with its background.  The minimum height and width for each
digit of the building number should be 50mm and 40mm respectively as shown below :-

New Buildings

5.
For new buildings, the developer or an Authorized Person  should take
into account the building numbering principles set down in RVD’s Practice Note 1/2007
during the design stage.

application in writing from a developer or an Authorized Person after the consent to
commence the building works has been given by the BA.  Upon completion of the new
building, the developer or the Authorized Person will be notified by the CRV of the
official building number.

Existing Buildings

8.
Where  any  alteration,  repair  or  renovation  works  affect  the  display  of
street  name  and  building  number,  the  Authorized  Person  should  ensure  that  any
obscured, displaced or damaged street name or building number are repaired or replaced
as soon as possible.
