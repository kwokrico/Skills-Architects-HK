Asbestos

Exposure to asbestos is hazardous to health.  As a general rule, all persons
involved in the design, construction, and maintenance of buildings must avoid the use of
asbestos containing material (ACM) and ensure that the removal of ACM from existing
buildings will be carried out only by registered asbestos personnel.

Prohibition

The use of asbestos and ACM in any industrial or manufacturing process
2.
including  any  building  operation  are  prohibited  under  the  Factories  and  Industrial
Undertakings  (Asbestos)  Regulation  (Cap  59AD).    Effective  from  4  April  2014,  the
import,  transhipment,  use  and  supply  of  asbestos  and  ACM  are  banned  under  the  Air
Pollution Control Ordinance (APCO) (Cap 311), whilst ACM present in existing buildings
will not be affected.
