# Cap 123N — Building (Minor Works) Regulation
**Architect critical summary for schematic design**  
Consolidated version: 31 March 2022 | Cap. 123 sub. leg. N

> Scope note: Cap 123N is the **Minor Works Control System (MWCS) rulebook** — the alternate path when small building works need **not** go through full Cap 123 s.14 plan approval + consent. It defines **what is minor works** (Sch. 1 Classes I–III / Types A–H), what is **designated exempted** (Sch. 2 — no notice/approval under s.41(3B)), who must be appointed (ss. 27–28), simplified submission packs (Part 6), supervision duties (Part 7), and Cap 123 **s.39C** validation of certain unauthorized works (Sch. 3). It is **not** the Building (Administration) plan package (Cap 123A), not volume/GFA control (Cap 123F), and not construction performance (Cap 123Q).

---

## Regulatory Overview

Cap 123N designates Class I / II / III minor works (and Types A–H by trade) for the purposes of Cap 123, and prescribes **simplified requirements** (Part 6) plus **designated exempted works** under Cap 123 s.41(3B). It applies whenever schematic design proposes A&A, signage, drainage, amenity structures, finishes, demolition of small unauthorized features, ventilation ducts, window works, or similar interventions that may fall inside Schedule 1 dimensional ladders instead of a full BD submission.

---

## Critical main topics and subtopics

### 1. First SD decision — pick the legal path by size / risk

At concept, sort **every** proposed intervention into **one** of five buckets. Crossing a Schedule number moves you up a class or out of MWCS entirely. A single package can mix buckets — each intervention is classified separately.

| Bucket | Legal effect | Who / notice | When to use |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Designated exempted** (Sch. 2) | Cap 123 s.14(1) **does not apply** (s.41(3B)) | No AP / MW notice for the exemption itself | Tiny low-risk works under Sch. 2 hard numbers |
| **Class III MW** | Simplified requirements; **post-completion** notice only | Registered contractor (item / Type) | Small works within Class III ceilings |
| **Class II MW** | **≥7 days** pre-start notice + plans by contractor | Registered contractor | Mid-band dimensional works |
| **Class I MW** | **≥7 days** pre-start notice + AP (or RI for prescribed repair) + RSE/RGE if triggered + plans | AP/RI + RSE/RGE as needed + registered contractor | Higher-risk / larger MW items |
| **Outside Sch. 1 / Sch. 2** | Full Cap 123 + Cap 123A approval / consent | AP / RSE / RGC as normal | Exceeds MW ceilings, loads cantilevered slabs illegally, alters protected structural systems, MOE stairs as stairs (not enclosure openings), etc. |

**Universal kill-switches (almost every MW item):**
- Do **not** put **additional load on any cantilevered slab** (unless the item expressly allows a limited case)
- Do **not** alter structural elements beyond the narrow carve-out: **simply supported beam** that is **not** prestressed and **not** used to support any column, flat slab or ribbed beam
- MOE / firefighting stairs themselves are **not** ordinary staircase items — only openings to their **non-loadbearing enclosure / protected lobby** are Class I (item **1.42**)
- Sch. 1 descriptions of erection include **replacement**; alteration includes **strengthening** and Cap 123 **s.39C(4)** rectification; removal includes **unauthorized** equivalents of that item (Sch. 1 §§2–6)

> SD takeaway: freeze projection depths, display areas, wall heights, excavation depths, AC plant weights, roof-edge setbacks, and “on cantilever vs not” **before** choosing MW vs full BP.

### 2. Who must be appointed (ss. 27–28) — lock the team at concept

#### 2.1 Class I — prescribed building professionals (s. 27)
| Role | Required when |
|---|---|
| **Authorized Person** | Always for Class I |
| **Registered Inspector** (assuming AP role) | Only if works are a **prescribed repair** or any **associated demolition works** |
| **Registered Structural Engineer** | Works involve **structural elements** |
| **Registered Geotechnical Engineer** | Works involve **geotechnical elements** |

**AP may prepare/sign Class I foundation / structural plans alone only if ALL of s.37(4) apply** (mirrors Cap 123A small-works logic):
- Construction height ≤ **10 m**
- Any structural span ≤ **6 m**
- Materials: timber / masonry / steel / plain or RC only
- Spread footings ≤ **300 kPa**, founded ≤ **2 m** below existing ground
- No alteration of existing RSE-designed structural elements

Otherwise RSE (and RGE for geo) must sign those sheets (s.37(1)).

#### 2.2 All classes — prescribed registered contractor (s. 28)
Appoint **one** of: RGBC; RMWC registered for the Type/item; or the specialist contractor the item demands:

|---|---|
| **Demolition** works category | **1.2, 1.5, 1.9, 1.10, 1.24, 1.30, 1.32, 1.33, 1.34, 1.37, 1.38, 1.39, 1.40**; **2.1, 2.2, 2.4, 2.9, 2.12, 2.24, 2.25, 2.26, 2.27, 2.31, 2.32, 2.37, 2.38, 2.39, 2.40, 2.63, 2.64, 2.65, 2.66, 2.67**; **3.1, 3.2, 3.4, 3.5, 3.7, 3.8, 3.18, 3.19, 3.20, 3.21, 3.22, 3.24, 3.26, 3.30, 3.32, 3.33, 3.43, 3.49, 3.63, 3.64, 3.65, 3.66** |
| **Site formation** | **1.11, 1.12, 2.10, 2.11, 3.53** |
| **Foundation** | **1.11, 1.12, 2.10, 2.11** |
| **Ground investigation field works** | **1.12, 2.11** |
| **Ventilation works** | **1.46, 1.51, 2.42, 2.46** |

**Class III natural-person RMWC** (registered under s.11 for specific items) must **personally** carry out the works (s.44) and certify that on the completion form (s.36).

**SD takeaway:** If the package includes slab openings, UBW removal, underground digs, or large ducts, budget the matching specialist category at fee proposal stage — RGBC/RMWC alone is not enough.

### 3. Simplified submission packs (Part 6) — programme hold points

#### 3.1 Class I — before commencement (s. 30) — ≥ **7 days**
AP / RI submits to BA:
1. **Specified-form notification** with:
   - Client naming each prescribed professional + contractor; each **confirms** appointment
   - AP/RI confirms **commencement date**
   - If repairs / A&A: AP/RI **and RSE (if any)** certify, after inspecting the building, that it can bear loads/stresses increased or altered by the works
   - If erecting a **signboard**: particulars of the person for whom it is erected
2. **Photographs** of existing physical condition
3. **Prescribed plans and details** (Cap 123A reg. 8 meaning via Cap 123N s.2)
4. **Supervision plan** if Technical Memorandum requires it

#### 3.2 Class I — on completion ≤ **14 days** (ss. 31–32)
| Non-demolition (s.31) | Demolition (s.32) |
|---|---|
| Revised plans if as-built differs | Same |
| Photos of completed works | Same |
| Cert: works per Ordinance + plans | Cert: works per Ordinance + plans |
| AP/RI: structurally safe | AP/RI: affected **land/street** has adequate **margin of safety**; remaining structure structurally safe |
| RSE (if appointed): structurally safe | RSE: MoS + remaining structure structurally safe |
| RGE (if appointed): geotechnically safe | RGE: MoS + remaining structure geotechnically safe |

#### 3.3 Class II — before commencement (s. 33) — ≥ **7 days**
**Contractor** (not AP) submits:
- Form: client names contractor; contractor confirms appointment + commencement date
- If A&A/repairs: contractor certifies building capacity after inspection
- Signboard particulars if erecting
- Photos of existing condition
- Prescribed plans and details

#### 3.4 Class II — completion ≤ **14 days** (ss. 34–35)
Contractor submits revised plans (if needed), photos, and certifies: works per Ordinance + plans; completion date; structurally safe (or for demo: MoS of affected land/street + remaining structure structurally safe).

#### 3.5 Class III — completion only (s. 36) — ≤ **14 days**
No pre-start BD notice. Contractor submits:
- Form: appointment + commence & complete dates; works per Ordinance + plans/description; personal-carry-out cert if s.11 RMWC; signboard particulars if erecting
- Plans or description of works as completed
- Photos **immediately before** and **after**

#### 3.6 Plan hygiene (ss. 37–39)
| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| Who signs Class I plans | AP/RI for architectural; RSE for structural (unless s.37(4) small-works); RGE for geo |
| Who signs Class II / III plans | Prescribed registered contractor |
| Scale | Plans/sections ≥ **1:100**; block plan ≥ **1:500** |
| Colour | Differentiate **existing vs new**, and parts of new work from each other |
| Clarity / medium | Clear & intelligible; hard-copy on durable material if paper |

### 5. Universal definitions that change numbers (Sch. 1 Part 1)

| Term | Design meaning |
|---|---|
| **Projecting signboard** | Fixed to external wall **and** projects **> 600 mm** |
| **Wall signboard** | Fixed to external wall; **no part** projects **> 600 mm** |
| **Display area** | Largest planar face of virtual rectangular prism containing the board (**exclude** slim single post Ø ≤ **100 mm**, or members solely for lateral restraint) |
| **Thickness (signboard)** | Smallest prism dimension of board + support (same exclusions) |
| **Flat** | Separate unit as delineated on approved plan (domestic or non-domestic) |
| **Aggregate length of additional wall** | Increase in total NL wall length (exclude door widths) vs approved plan — flat or roof |
| **Building services installation** | Includes solar WH, PV, antenna, transceiver, AC unit, cooling tower, light fitting, pump set + associated ducts; **excludes** water tank, lift, stairlift, lifting platform, ventilation duct, radio base station |
| **Inaccessible roof** | Not for human occupation; access only for M&R personnel |
| **Private / non-private garden** | Private = not common part, or exclusive use of an owner |
| **Planter** | Fixed planter wall or fixed soil-containing feature (not slope soil) |
| **Trellis** | Overhead frame for growing plants |
| **Roof finishes** | Tile layer, screeding layer and associated waterproofing on the roof |
| **Cross-sectional dimension** (ducts) | Internal h / w / Ø on a **vertical** CS when the duct is laid horizontally (Sch. 1 s.1A) — used for items **1.46, 1.47, 2.46, 3.48** and Sch. 2 item **23** |

##### 6.7a Retractable awning — full Class II gates (item **2.43**)
All must be true:
- No other structural alteration
- Opening is a **door** (not escape-stair exit, not to balcony/verandah) **or** a **window** (not plant room / lavatory / bathroom / kitchen)
- **Not** fixed to any cantilevered slab
- No part above the **ceiling of that storey**
- Highest point ≤ **5.5 m** above ground (or roof if above a roof)
- Retracted project ≤ **500 mm**; fully extended ≤ **2 m** over a roof or ≤ **2.5 m** otherwise
- Awning ≤ **500 mm** wider than opening on **each** side

### 11. Schematic design checklist (use before freezing the package)

1. **List every intervention** (wall, opening, projection, plant, drain, sign, dig, fence, window, cladding, UBW removal).
2. **Tag location risk:** cantilevered slab? MOE stair enclosure? inaccessible roof? street / common part projection? slope / RW within setback?
3. **Measure against Sch. 2** → if fully inside, book as designated exempted.
4. Else walk **Class III → II → I** ladders in §6; stop at first matching item; record the item number on the SD compliance sheet.
5. If any kill-switch fails (extra cantilever load, protected structure, size overrun, last manhole, escape-stair door awning, etc.) → **full Cap 123A** path.
6. Appoint **AP/RSE/RGE/specialist/RMWC** per §2; align contractor **Types A–H** per §4.
7. Put **≥7-day** Class I/II notices, capacity inspections, and plan production on the programme; Class III = completion pack only.
8. For flat A&A: compute **aggregate additional wall m per m²** and screed density/patch rules; flag **1.41** if creating ≥3 wet sleeping rooms.
9. For existing UBW amenity / signs / fences → test **Sch. 3 / s.39C** (and cut-off dates) before assuming illegal demolition or full BP.
10. Still verify **lease, OZP Notes, FS Code, PNAP, Cap 123F GFA**, and adjoining-owner interfaces — Cap 123N never waives those (ss. 60–61).
