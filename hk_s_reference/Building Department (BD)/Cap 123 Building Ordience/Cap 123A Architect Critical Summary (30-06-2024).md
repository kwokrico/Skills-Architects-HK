# Cap 123A — Building (Administration) Regulations
**Architect critical summary for schematic design**  
Consolidated version: 30 June 2024 | Cap. 123 sub. leg. A

> Scope note: Cap 123A is the **BD submission, signing, consent, and certification rulebook** — not planning volume control (that is Cap 123F) or construction quality (Cap 123Q). Use this before schematic design to lock consultant roles, plan packages, scales, programme hold points, and site-formation / scheduled-area triggers.

---

## Regulatory Overview

Cap 123A governs how all building works and street works under the Buildings Ordinance are **documented, signed, submitted for BA approval, consented for commencement, supervised, and certified on completion**. It applies to projects that require AP / RSE / RGE / registered contractor appointment under Cap 123; it **does not apply** to minor works commenced under the simplified requirements (reg. 48).

## Critical main topics and subtopics

### 1. Who must sign what (lock team roles at concept stage)

| Document type | Who must prepare & sign | Schematics implication |
|---|---|---|
| Architectural / general building plans, drainage, block/key, PR-SC diagram, BFA plans, sanitary schedules | **Authorized Person (AP)** | AP owns overall compliance certificate (reg. 18A) |
| Foundation plans, structural details & calculations | **Registered Structural Engineer (RSE)** — unless *all* of the small-works exemptions in reg. 12(4) apply | Budget RSE from day one for anything >10 m high, spans >6 m, or deep / piled foundations |
| Geotech assessment, site formation, ELS, GI plans/reports, site investigation & related calcs | **Registered Geotechnical Engineer (RGE)** | Trigger RGE early if slopes, retention, bulk excavation, or scheduled areas |
| Stability certificate for A&A / repairs | AP; **RSE + AP** if the existing building needed an RSE originally | Do not start A&A schema without existing structure capacity check (reg. 18) |

**AP may sign structural/foundation docs alone only when ALL of these are true (reg. 12(4)):**
- Construction height ≤ **10 m**
- Any structural span ≤ **6 m**
- Materials: timber / masonry / steel / plain or RC only
- Spread footings ≤ **300 kPa**, founding ≤ **2 m** below existing ground
- No alteration to existing structural elements designed by an RSE

**Conflict rule (reg. 5 / 5A):** AP / RSE / RGE / RI must disclose in writing to the client any contractor / supplier interest or commission.  
**One team rule (reg. 15):** BA may refuse plans from two or more APs / RSEs / RGEs for the same premises.

### 2. Prescribed plan package for new building works (reg. 8(1)) — design checklist

Design schematic so every future BD sheet has a clear home. Mandatory / typically required set:

#### 2.1 Core architectural / planning set
- **Floor & roof plans** — full dimensions; wall thickness; windows; soil/waste fitments; water tanks; **intended use of every part**
- **Elevations + levels** — all elevations; adjacent street levels vs known datum & site/building levels; street widths; any well (position, depth, construction)
- **Sections** — foundations; site & lowest floor levels; **building height**; **clear storey heights**; windows & floors; adjoining roof levels; max boundary-to-boundary gradient; **max excavation depth**; **max fill thickness**; excavation & fill volumes
- **Drainage plan** — pipe sizes/depths; public sewer/drain/nullah connections (or other disposal); disconnecting traps; ventilation
- **Block plan** (≥ 1:500) — building size/position; nearby buildings; neighbouring streets
- **Key plan** — if site not obvious from block plan
- **PR / SC / height diagram (reg. 8(1)(h))** — with full dimensions & calculations showing height, site coverage, plot ratio & disposition per Cap 123F regs 19–21; plus open space per Cap 123F regs 25, 27, 28
- **Sanitary / UFA details (reg. 8(1)(k))** — usable floor area (per Cap 123I); number & type of soil and waste fitments
- **Barrier-free access plans (reg. 8(1)(m))** — where Cap 123F reg. 72 applies; show DMBA provisions in detail
- **Street projections (reg. 8(1)(j))** — canopy / bridge / projection over street or unleased Gov land — nature & dimensions

#### 2.2 Structural / foundation set
- **Foundation plan** — size & position of foundations; full site investigation report; any proposed ground grouting
- **Structural details + one set of structural calculations**
- **Scheduled areas 2 or 4:** foundation submission must also include the full geo supporting pack (guide, desk study, GI, groundwater monitoring, interpretation + design assumptions, foundation calcs)

#### 2.3 Geotechnical / earthworks set (when triggered)
| Trigger | Prescribed deliverable | Design / programme alert |
|---|---|---|
| BA requires it | **Geotechnical assessment** of site adequacy accompanying floor / elev / section / PR plans; include site formation & foundation plans/sections | Assume needed on sloping / retained / filled / complicated sites |
| Site formation works | **Site formation plan** + full supporting reports (A–F): topo/geology/groundwater study; GI; piezometer monitoring; interpretation + design assumptions + contingency if assumption fails (must still satisfy Cap 123Q s.21); stability analyses & calcs (paginated + indexed) | Sequence, methods, heavy-rain safety precautions, schedule of geo **design assumptions** must be designed in — not afterthoughts |
| Scheduled Area **No. 1** (bulk excavation) | SF plan must show **envelope of all bulk excavation** (cross-sections + plan contours with levels above HKPD) | Massing / basement extents are BA-controlled envelopes |
| BA requires it | **Excavation & lateral support (ELS) plan** + supporting pack (A–F), demonstrating Cap 123Q s.14 compliance; monitoring of building/ground movement & piezometric levels | Basement / deep dig must set retention, dewatering, ground treatment & monitoring strategy at concept |
| Scheduled Areas | **Ground investigation plan** — exploratory locations/depths or selection criteria; lab testing criteria; equipment/procedures | Do not start schematic foundations without GI plan path |
| Scheduled Area **No. 1** | **Foundation plan must be submitted together with site formation plan** (reg. 11A) | Cannot split SF approval from foundation on Area 1 |

#### 2.4 Demolition package (reg. 8(3)–(4)) — if strip-out / demo is part of the scheme
Plans for: existing construction & slopes; survey of surrounds; sequence / plant / procedures; temporary support stage-by-stage; public protection (dust screens, catch fans, hoardings, covered walkways); support to affected neighbours.  
Accompanied by: stability reports for demo building & affected assets; support calcs if BA requires; calcs proving adequate margin of safety & that plant use will not damage neighbours.

#### 2.5 Street works package (reg. 9)
Key plan (≥ 1:2 500); layout (≥ 1:200); longitudinal sections (H ≥ 1:500, V ≥ 1:200); cross sections (≥ 1:200); typical road sections (≥ 1:50); foundation + structural details/calcs for street structures.

### 3. Drawing standards that kill first submissions (regs. 13–14)

| Item | Rule |
|---|---|
| Building plans & sections | Scale **not less than 1:100** (BA may accept 1:200 only for very extensive works) |
| Block plan | ≥ **1:500** |
| Colouring | **One copy** coloured to differentiate existing vs new, and parts of new work from each other |
| Clarity | Clear, intelligible; hard-copy on suitable durable material; **no ferro-prussic** engineering prints |
| Hard-copy submissions | In **duplicate**; BA may demand more copies (reg. 11) |
| BA extras | BA can demand enlarged details, water-supply info, special materials specs, or any other particulars (reg. 10) |

### 4. Approval & consent timelines (regs. 29–34) — programme constraints

| Action | Deemed period (Cap 123 s.15 + Cap 123A) | Architect note |
|---|---|---|
| First plan submission / **major revision** | **60 days** for deemed approval | BD may notify “major revision” within **30 days** of re-submission |
| Re-submission of same plans (not major) | **30 days** | |
| Consent to commence (any part of approved works) | **28 days** deemed | Partial consent possible |
| Alteration of consented works | Re-apply with amended plans + original consent notice; **28 days** | Amendment fees may apply |
| Plans may be submitted **separately** | Allowed (reg. 29(2)) | **Exception:** Area 1 — foundation **must** accompany site formation (reg. 11A) |
| Further particulars after BA refusal under Cap 123 s.16 | Treated as **fresh application** (reg. 29(3)) | Watch fee & clock resets |

Approval is signified by written notice **and** stamp/sign/date on the plan (not on structural/geo calcs). Stamp-approved copies go back to AP / RSE / RGE (reg. 30).

### 5. Start / stop / change-of-team hold points (regs. 19–24, 28)

| Event | Deadline / effect |
|---|---|
| Notice of contractor appointment + contractor acceptance / commencement date (**Form — reg. 20**) | **≥ 7 days before** works start |
| If supervision plan required: notify TCP appointments + CVs (AP/RSE/RGE side + contractor side) | **≥ 7 days before** start |
| AP / RSE / RGE ceases engagement | Notify BA in writing within **7 days** |
| Change of AP / RSE / RGE / contractor | Client notifies BA within **7 days**; **works of that discipline must stop** until replacement appointed & BA notified |
| TCP ceases | That part of works under the TCP **stops** until replaced |
| Emergency works engagement | Certify on Cap 123 s.19 form within **48 hours** |
| Appointment of AP / RSE / RGE | Client + appointee confirmation on specified form (reg. 23) |

### 6. Supervision duties once on site (Part 5) — fee / team implications at SD

- **AP** — periodical supervision so works generally follow Ordinance, approved plans, supervision plan (Technical Memorandum under Cap 123 s.39A), and BA conditions (reg. 37)
- **RSE / RGE** — same, for structural / geotechnical works
- **Registered contractor** — **continuous** supervision; keep approved plans **and** supervision plans on site (regs. 40–41); keep supervision records **≥ 12 months** after final completion certificate
- AP must supply stamped approved plans (+ structural/geo details) to the contractor (reg. 36)
- BA may reject/revoke TCPs whose qualifications/experience are insufficient
- Extra BA inspections for contraventions / wrong info → AP/RSE/RGE pay prescribed fee (reg. 39)

### 7. Completion & OP-path certificates (regs. 25–26, 25A)

| Party | Timing | Certifies |
|---|---|---|
| Registered contractor | Within **7 days** of completion | Works built per Ordinance / regs / approved plans → deliver to AP (or RSE/RGE as applicable) |
| AP | Within **14 days** of completion | Compliance + **structurally safe** (opinion) → send to BA |
| RSE / RGE (if engaged) | Within **7 days** of receiving contractor cert | Structural / geot safety opinion → AP → BA within further **7 days** |
| Demolition completion | AP / RSE / RGE within **14 days** | Demo per approved plans; remaining structures safe; affected land/street adequate margin of safety |
| Street works | Same 7 / 14 day pattern | Per approved plans |
| **Water supply (reg. 25A)** — new buildings with WC / latrine / urinal **or** waste fitments / showers | AP must send **with** reg. 25(2) cert | Permanent WSD connection cert from Water Authority (**issued within 10 days** of written application), **or** well / alternative supply certs if BA permitted under Cap 123I reg. 10A |

> Schematic implication: coordinate WSD connection design and programme; OP path dies without water certs where sanitary / waste fittings are provided.

### 8. Fees that affect submission strategy (reg. 42 — selected)

| Item | Practical takeaway |
|---|---|
| Plan resubmission / amendment of approved plans (items 7) | Generally **free** when it is a further/amendment submission under regs. 6(2), 29, 33 |
| New plan or **major revision** | Charged by **GFA bands** (industrial vs non-industrial) or by sheet size for A&A / non-accountable GFA structures |
| Non-industrial new building ≤ 10,000 m² GFA | $4,340 / 100 m² (min $10,350) |
| Non-industrial new building > 10,000 m² GFA | $3,480 / 100 m² (min $434,400) |
| Industrial ≤ 20,000 m² / > 20,000 m² | Separate lower bands (min $10,400 / $547,100) |
| A&A / works not resulting in a new building | $14,200 per A1 (841 × 594 mm) or part |
| Bona fide misinterpretation of Ordinance | BA may waive further-submission fee under reg. 6(2) |

*(Fee amounts as in the 30.6.2024 consolidated text; confirm current GAZEX if quoting commercially.)*

### 9. Definitions that change site strategy (reg. 2)

- **Bulk excavation** — all excavation **except** GI, public utility trenches, drains, sewers, piles, or caissons. In Scheduled Area 1 this drives the excavation envelope drawing.
- **Geotechnical design assumption** — the geology / groundwater / site history / adjacent foundations / shear strength / effect of works, etc., used to prove **adequate margin of safety**. Wrong assumptions force designed contingencies (must still meet Cap 123Q s.14 / s.21).
- **Site formation plans** — those prescribed in reg. 8.

### 10. Schematic-design go / no-go gate (architect checklist)

Before freezing massing and consultant briefs:

1. **Confirm BO pathway** — full Cap 123 process vs minor works simplified route (reg. 48 exemption).
2. **Map scheduled area number** — Area 1 couples SF + foundation; Areas 2/4 thicken foundation geo packs; GI plan mandatory for scheduled areas.
3. **Appoint / budget AP + RSE + RGE** using the reg. 12 signing matrix; resolve conflict-of-interest disclosures.
4. **List every reg. 8 sheet** that the massing will eventually need (esp. PR-SC diagram, BFA, drainage outfall, ELS/basement envelope, projections over streets).
5. **Set floor-to-floor / height narrative early** — plans must declare height and clear storey heights.
6. **Assume 60-day first approval + 28-day consent** on the programme; separate package strategy except where reg. 11A forbids it.
7. **Bake in SI / GI / groundwater monitoring** if any retention, fill, slope, or deep dig is possible.
8. **Plan OP cert chain** — contractor → AP/RSE/RGE → BA, plus Water Authority connection certs.
9. **If demo / A&A** — commission stability cert (reg. 18) and demo plan set before stripping assumptions enter the schema.
10. **Scale & colour discipline** — 1:100 (or agreed 1:200), coloured exist/new, no ferro prints.
