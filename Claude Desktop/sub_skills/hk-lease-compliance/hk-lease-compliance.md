# HK Lease Compliance
Covers the contractual relationship with the Government as Landlord, Lands Department (LandsD) protocols, Lease Modification/Waivers, and specific GFA/Height/User restrictions found in Land Grants and New Grants.

---

## 1. The Three-Tiered Regulatory Framework
In Hong Kong project delivery, we must satisfy three distinct (and often conflicting) regimes:
1.  **Lease (LandsD):** The contractual agreement between the Government and the Grantee.
2.  **Town Planning (PlanD):** Statutory restrictions under the Outlook Zoning Plan (OZP).
3.  **Buildings Ordinance (BD):** Safety, health, and structural standards (Cap. 123).

> **Rico’s Note:** Always remember that "GFA" under Lease may be defined differently than "GFA" under PNAP APP-2. LandsD is not bound by BD’s exemptions unless the Lease explicitly states "as defined under the Buildings Ordinance."

---

## 2. Lease Modification & Waiver Types

| Mechanism | Purpose | Process |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lease Modification** | Permanent change to lease terms (e.g., changing use from Industrial to Residential). | Requires Premium payment; involve LandsD District Lands Office (DLO). |
| **Temporary Waiver** | Short-term permission to breach a lease covenant (e.g., retail use in an industrial unit). | Annual waiver fee; subject to 3-year or 7-year terms. |
| **Modification by Letter** | Minor technical changes or "No Objection" letters for specific design features. | Usually involves administrative fees rather than full premiums. |

---

## 3. Key Lease Restrictions vs. BD/OZP

| Feature | Lease Condition (LandsD) | OZP / Buildings Ordinance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **GFA Calculation** | Often "Gross Floor Area" includes everything under the roof unless "deductible" is specified. | Specific exemptions per PNAP APP-2 (e.g., plant rooms, car parks). |
| **Building Height** | Usually expressed in "Metres above Hong Kong Principal Datum" (mPD). | Measured from mean street level or as per BHR in OZP. |
| **User Clause** | Strictly limited (e.g., "Private Residential Purposes only"). | More flexible (e.g., "Residential Group A" allows some retail). |
| **Tree Preservation** | Extremely strict; requires Tree Survey and Removal Permit (TRP). | Primarily focused on site safety and slope stability. |
| **Design & Disposition** | Clause often requires "Design, Disposition, and Height" (DD&H) approval. | General compliance with Building (Planning) Regs. |

## 4. LandsD Submission & Approval Workflow

### 4.1 Master Layout Plan (MLP) Submission
For large-scale developments (e.g., MTR TODs or Comprehensive Development Areas), the Lease often requires MLP approval from the Director of Lands.
* **Key Items:** Internal transport arrangements, GFA breakdown, open space provision, and landscape master plans (LMP).
* **Coordination:** Must be consistent with the S.16 Planning Approval if applicable.

### 4.2 Certificate of Compliance (C of C)
The final step after the Occupation Permit (OP) is issued by BD.
* **Prerequisite:** All lease conditions (including public open space, road works, and tree planting) must be fulfilled.
* **Financial Impact:** Without a C of C, the developer cannot execute the legal assignment of individual units (i.e., they cannot sell).

---

## 5. Technical Coordination Checklists

### 5.1 GFA/Plot Ratio Discrepancies
* **Check:** Does the lease define GFA as per the Building (Planning) Regulations?
* **Action:** If not, clarify with DLO whether green features (balconies) or plant rooms will be "premium-earning" GFA.

### 5.2 Parking Requirements
* **Check:** Does the Lease specify a *minimum* or *maximum* number of spaces?
* **Conflict:** BD/PlanD might require fewer spaces than the Lease. A **Lease Variation** or **Waiver** may be needed to align with the HKPSG (Hong Kong Planning Standards and Guidelines).

### 5.3 Sustainable Building Design (SBD) Guidelines
* **PNAP APP-152:** Compliance is required for GFA concessions under BD.
* **Lease Implication:** Ensure that building setbacks or voids created for SBD compliance do not violate "Non-Building Areas" (NBA) specified in the Lease.

---

## 6. Common Terms & Acronyms
* **DLO:** District Lands Office.
* **Premium:** The "market value" difference paid to the Government for enhancing land use rights.
* **STW:** Short Term Waiver.
* **STT:** Short Term Tenancy (for use of adjoining Government land).
* **Mod Letter:** Modification Letter.
