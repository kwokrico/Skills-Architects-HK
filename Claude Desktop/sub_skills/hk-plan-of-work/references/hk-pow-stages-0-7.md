# HK Plan of Work — Full Checklists (RIBA Stages 0–7)

Bilingual task checklists localised for Hong Kong. Apply substitutions in `hk-plan-of-work` Section 3 where UK terms appear. Cross-references: `skill_id` in **HK note** lines.

---

## STAGE 0 — Strategic Definition | 戰略定義

**HK reminders:** PI insurance (`hk-professional-indemnity`); fee basis (`hk-fee-proposal-strategy`); conflict check; OZP/lease desktop study if site known.

### 0.1 Client Team | 客戶團隊

- [ ] **0.1.1 Client Background | 客戶背景**
  - Investigate client's business record and financial position
  - Review previous projects (HK projects where possible)
  - Consult other consultants about working with the client
- [ ] **0.1.2 Client Representative | 客戶代表**
  - Identify client's status and authority
  - Ensure single point of decision-making to prevent miscommunication
- [ ] **0.1.3 Project Nature | 項目性質**
  - Determine if project is for direct occupation, sale, or lease
  - Identify potential third-party involvement (landlord, fund, operator)
  - Consider extra work for community or first-time developer projects
- [ ] **0.1.4 Client Experience | 客戶體驗**
  - Assess client's familiarity with construction projects in HK
  - Be cautious with inexperienced clients
  - Understand legal duty of care (`hk-professional-indemnity`)
- [ ] **0.1.5 Fee Proposal | 費用建議**
  - Carefully price client requirements
  - Be aware of HK market conditions
  - Propose a fee that ensures professional service
  - Negotiate realistically — `hk-fee-proposal-strategy`
- [ ] **0.1.6 Professional Services Role | 專業服務角色**
  - Identify specific role (lead designer, project lead, consultant, AP, etc.)
  - Consider implications of the role (statutory vs non-statutory)
- [ ] **0.1.7 Appointment Terms | 預約條款**
  - Carefully examine appointment terms (HKIA/HKIS form, not RIBA PSC)
  - Negotiate if necessary
  - Seek legal and insurance advice if needed
- [ ] **0.1.8 Professional Conflicts | 職業衝突**
  - Check conflicts with HKIA Code of Conduct and practice rules
  - Verify no competition with existing clients
- [ ] **0.1.9 Contract Recommendations | 合約推薦**
  - Recommend appropriate **consultant appointment** (Archi-SOR / HKIA agreement)
  - Clearly define included and excluded services
  - Ensure clear communication with clients
  - *HK note:* Building contract form advice is pre-design — flag SFBC/GCC/D&B for later (`hk-tender-contract-administration`)
- [ ] **0.1.10 Practice Resources | 實踐資源**
  - Assess required skills and staff availability
  - Consider subcontracting specialist inputs if needed
  - Comply with HKIA registration and local practice requirements
- [ ] **0.1.11 Commission Feasibility | 佣金可行性**
  - Evaluate timetable, service quality, and budget
  - Assess risks and potential benefits
- [ ] **0.1.12 Commission Acceptance | 委託受理**
  - Decide whether to accept the commission
  - Confirm in writing
- [ ] **0.1.13 Appointment Documents | 預約文件**
  - Submit documents for signature
  - Use preliminary agreement if formal agreement is premature
- [ ] **0.1.14 Fee Calculation | 費用計算**
  - Consider work programme
  - Calculate time and cost
  - Document speculative work basis
- [ ] **0.1.15 Insurance Considerations | 保險注意事項**
  - Check professional indemnity insurance coverage (`hk-professional-indemnity`)
  - Verify subconsultant competence and PI

### 0.2 Cost | 成本

- [ ] **0.2.1 Cost Parameters | 成本參數**
  - Understand likely capital cost (HK $/m² benchmarks)
  - Seek advice from QS or cost consultant if necessary (`hk-cost-consultancy`)

### 0.3 Other Activities | 其他活動

- [ ] **0.3.1 Previous Involvement | 以前的參與**
  - Check termination of previous architect/consultant appointments
  - Notify previous architects of new appointment where appropriate
- [ ] **0.3.2 Record Keeping | 記錄保存**
  - Maintain systematic and comprehensive communication records
  - Ensure secure backup arrangements

### 0.4 BIM and Digital Technology | BIM與數碼科技

- [ ] **0.4.1 BIM Implementation | BIM實施**
  - Advise client on BIM purpose and benefits
  - Agree on BIM usage extent (HK public works often require BIM)
- [ ] **0.4.2 Team Integration | 團隊整合**
  - Advise on integrated team services
  - Identify specialist and information manager requirements
- [ ] **0.4.3 Responsibility Matrix | 責任矩陣**
  - Prepare draft Responsibility Matrix
  - Clarify design information contributions
  - Ensure design coordination
