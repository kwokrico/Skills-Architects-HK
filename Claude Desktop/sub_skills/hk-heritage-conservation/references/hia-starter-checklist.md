## 5) Sign-off Checklist Before Submission

- [ ] All CDEs identified, mapped, and cross-referenced in drawings
- [ ] Impact assessment completed for each intervention touching CDEs
- [ ] Mitigation measures linked to each impact item
- [ ] Reversibility and material compatibility rationale documented
- [ ] Authority consultation log updated and unresolved comments clearly flagged
- [ ] Submission index and revision history complete
- [ ] Internal QA sign-off complete (AP + heritage specialist + discipline leads)
