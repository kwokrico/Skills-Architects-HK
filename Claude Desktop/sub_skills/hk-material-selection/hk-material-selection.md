# HK Material Selection

Climate: subtropical marine (CZ 1A). Performance target: BEAM Plus NB Materials credits. Durability driven by salt-laden air, high humidity, and typhoon wind-driven rain.

---

## 1. BEAM Plus Materials Credits

| Credit | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Recycled content | ≥ 10% by value of total materials |
| Regional materials | ≥ 20% by value sourced within 800 km (covers Mainland China) |
| FSC timber | ≥ 50% of wood-based products FSC-certified |
| Low-VOC paints | VOC ≤ 50 g/L (flat); ≤ 150 g/L (non-flat) |
| Low-VOC adhesives/sealants | VOC ≤ 70 g/L (flooring); ≤ 250 g/L (construction) |
| Rapidly renewable | Bamboo, cork, linoleum where applicable |

---

## 2. Facade Material Durability (Marine Exposure)

| Material | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| Facade framing | Aluminium alloy 6063-T5 or 6061-T6; anodised or PVDF coated |
| Fixings | Grade 316 stainless steel minimum; Grade 316L for coastal sites |
| Sealants | Neutral-cure silicone; 10-yr replacement cycle; UV-stable |
| GRC panels | Common in HK; lightweight; good durability; verify alkali-resistant glass fibre |
| Natural stone | Granite (local/Mainland China); verify salt resistance; back-ventilated preferred |
| Mild steel | Not acceptable exposed; hot-dip galvanised + paint system minimum |

---

## 3. Regional Sourcing (HK Context)

| Material | Typical Source |
|---|---|
| Granite / stone | Fujian, Guangdong (within 800 km) |
| Ceramic / porcelain tiles | Guangdong, Fujian |
| Aluminium extrusions | Guangdong (major production hub) |
| Precast concrete | HK local fabricators |
| Structural steel | Mainland China mills (within 800 km) |
| Timber (FSC) | Verify chain of custody; FSC-certified suppliers in Mainland China available |

---

## 4. Typhoon Impact Resistance

| Element | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Cladding fixings | Designed to HKWC 2004 wind pressures; SS fixings |
| Glazing | Impact-resistant laminated glass for typhoon-exposed facades |
| Roof finishes | Mechanically fixed; avoid loose ballast on exposed roofs |
| Signage / louvres | Designed for typhoon wind load; secure fixing details required |

---

## 5. Internal Finishes (Subtropical Humidity)

| Issue | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| Mould resistance | Low-VOC anti-mould paint in bathrooms, kitchens, and poorly ventilated spaces |
| Timber flooring | Engineered timber preferred over solid; acclimate before installation |
| Carpet | Avoid in high-humidity areas; specify mould-resistant backing |
| Concrete floors | Epoxy or polyurethane coating for car parks; anti-slip finish for ramps |

---

*Sources: BEAM Plus NB v2.0 Materials credits, HKWC 2004, HKGBC material guidelines, EPD VOC guidelines.*
