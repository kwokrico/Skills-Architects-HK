## 2. HK Quantitative Rules of Thumb

### 2.1 Floor-to-Floor Heights (Buildings Ordinance context)

| Building Type | Typical F-F Height | BO / PNAP Basis | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Domestic (residential) | 2.9 – 3.1 m | BO Reg. 30: min 2.5m headroom | GFA-exempt features (bay windows, planters) affect slab-to-slab |
| Non-domestic (office) | 3.8 – 4.2 m | BO Reg. 30: min 3.0m headroom | Raised floor + ceiling void for M&E |
| Retail (ground floor) | 4.5 – 5.5 m | PNAP APP-2 | Double-height retail common in podiums |
| Car park (within building) | 2.3 – 2.5 m (clear) | BO Reg. 46 | Min 2.0m clear per BO; 2.3m practical |
| Plant room | 3.5 – 5.0 m | Project-specific | Coordinate with BD submission |

### 2.3 GFA Exemptions (PNAP APP-2 key items)

| Feature | GFA Exemption | Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| Bay window | Exempt up to 0.5m projection, ≤50% of facade width per floor | Must be non-habitable, open on ≥1 side |
| Balcony | Exempt up to 2m depth, ≤1/2 of unit facade | Open balcony; aggregate cap per building |
| Utility platform | Exempt (≤1.5m × 2.0m per unit) | Kitchen/laundry use only |
| Covered walkway / arcade | Exempt if publicly accessible | Per PNAP APP-2 conditions |
| Plant room / lift overrun | Exempt | Must not exceed BO height limits |
| Caretaker unit | Exempt (≤35m² per building) | One per building |

### 2.4 Building Height Restriction (BHR)

- BHR defined in OZP in metres above Principal Datum (mPD) or storeys
- Check OZP annotation for site-specific BHR before any massing study
- Aviation height restrictions (CAAS): consult for Kowloon, Lantau, airport environs
- Ridgeline protection: consult HKPSG Chapter 11 and relevant OZP

## 3. HK Building Typology Taxonomy

### 3.1 High-Density Residential

| Sub-type | Key Design Drivers | Critical Metrics |
|---|---|---|
| **HA Public Housing (Harmony/Non-standard)** | Unit mix per HA schedule, cross-ventilation, communal facilities ratio | 35–40 m² (1P1R) to 70 m² (4P2R); 6–8 units/core |
| **HD Public Housing** | Similar to HA; HD manages rental estates | Per HD allocation standards |
| **Private residential tower** | GFA exemptions maximised, saleable area ratio, view corridors | Net-to-gross 75–82%; plot ratio per OZP |
| **Village house (NT Exempted House)** | 3-storey max, 65.03 m² per floor, 66.7% site coverage | NTEH policy; no lift required |
| **Composite building** | Residential over commercial podium; structural transfer slab | Transfer slab at L3–L5; separate residential lobby |

### 3.2 MTR Transit-Oriented Development (TOD)

| Aspect | Typical Approach |
|---|---|
| Rail + Property integration | MTR Corp as co-developer; above-station development rights |
| Podium-tower typology | Retail podium (L1–L5) + residential/office towers above |
| Pedestrian connectivity | Covered walkway network linking to MTR concourse |
| Plot ratio bonus | Possible under OZP for public transport interchange provision |

### 3.3 Commercial & Mixed-Use

| Sub-type | Key Drivers | HK-Specific Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Grade A office tower** | BEAM Plus, curtain wall OTTV, floor plate 1,500–2,500 m² | Central/Kowloon East submarkets; BD non-domestic PR |
| **Shopping mall / podium retail** | Anchor tenant strategy, atrium daylight, servicing | Linked to MTR; FEHD licensing for F&B |
| **Hotel** | Tourism Commission grading, FEHD, fire cert | 28–42 m²/key; BOH 15–20% GFA |

## 4. Design Quality Framework (HK Context)

### 4.1 Regulatory Compliance Checklist

- [ ] OZP checked: zone, plot ratio, site coverage, BHR, special restrictions
- [ ] BO Cap. 123 compliance: headroom, natural lighting/ventilation (Reg. 30), means of escape (Reg. 41)
- [ ] PNAP APP-2: GFA exemptions claimed correctly; aggregate balcony cap not exceeded
- [ ] Fire Services Dept (FSD) consultation: sprinkler, hose reel, means of escape per FS Code
- [ ] Drainage: stormwater and foul drainage per DSD standards; SUDS where applicable
- [ ] Barrier-free access: DMBA 2008 compliance throughout
- [ ] BEAM Plus target credit level confirmed at concept stage (Provisional → Final)

### 4.2 Environmental Performance (HK Subtropical CZ 1A)

- [ ] OTTV ≤ 20 W/m² (non-domestic); RTTV ≤ 25 W/m²
- [ ] External shading designed for E/W facades (horizontal fins or deep reveals)
- [ ] Natural ventilation feasibility assessed before mechanical-only solution
- [ ] Roof: reflective coating or green roof to reduce urban heat island
- [ ] Typhoon resistance: glazing and cladding designed for wind load per HKWC (Code of Practice on Wind Effects)

### 4.3 Urban Design & Context

- [ ] Air ventilation assessment (AVA) commissioned if required by PlanD
- [ ] Building setback and permeability per HKPSG urban design guidelines
- [ ] Podium height ≤ 15 m to maintain street-level human scale
- [ ] View corridor to harbour or ridgeline not obstructed (check OZP/HKPSG Ch.11)
- [ ] Active frontage at ground floor; blank walls minimised

## 5. HK Anti-Pattern Catalog

| Anti-Pattern | Failure Mode | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| **Ignoring GFA exemption aggregates** | Balcony exemptions exceed PNAP APP-2 aggregate cap; BD rejects plans | Calculate aggregate balcony GFA early; track against cap per floor and per building |
| **Plot ratio calculated without GFA exemptions** | Saleable area underestimated; project not viable | Distinguish gross floor area (BO definition) from saleable area (SRPE); model both |
| **Single-aspect units facing west** | Overheating in subtropical climate; BEAM Plus IEQ credits lost | Dual-aspect preferred; west-facing units require external shading or high-performance glass (SHGC ≤ 0.25) |
| **Transfer slab not coordinated early** | Structural transfer at podium/tower interface conflicts with M&E risers and unit layouts above | Fix transfer level at concept; coordinate grid, riser positions, and unit module simultaneously |
| **Means of escape non-compliant** | Travel distance or exit width fails BO Reg. 41 / FS Code; BD/FSD objection | Check MOE at every floor plan iteration; max travel distance 45 m (sprinklered residential) |
| **Village house exceeding NTEH limits** | BD rejects; unauthorized structure risk | Strictly enforce 3-storey, 65.03 m²/floor, 8.23 m height, 66.7% site coverage |
| **BEAM Plus left to end of design** | Credits requiring early design decisions (orientation, shading, materials) missed | Appoint BEAM Pro at concept; map credits to RIBA/HKIA stages |
| **AVA ignored in dense urban site** | PlanD requires AVA retrospectively; massing redesign required | Commission AVA at feasibility/concept for sites in AVA-sensitive areas (per PlanD guidance) |
